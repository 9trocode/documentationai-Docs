## Overview

Create your Pipeops account, verify your email address, and set up your workspace so you are ready to choose a deployment path.

<Callout kind="info">

You only need to complete registration once. After that, you sign in with the same provider or email to access your existing workspace.

</Callout>

## Register your Pipeops account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Pipeops home page" icon="home">

    Go to `https://pipeops.io` in your browser. From the home page, access the console to start registration.

    <Image
      src="https://docs.pipeops.io/img/docs/user-registration/landing-page.png"
      alt="Pipeops marketing homepage with a call-to-action to access the console"
      width="1440"
      height="900"
    />

  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the registration page" icon="arrow-right">

    Open the registration page directly at `https://console.pipeops.io/auth/signup`, or click the sign-up button from the console entry point.

    <Image
      src="https://docs.pipeops.io/img/docs/user-registration/landing-page-sign-up.png"
      alt="Pipeops console sign-up screen with a prominent Sign Up button"
      width="1440"
      height="900"
    />

  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a sign-up method" icon="users">

    Select how you want to create your account. You can register using:

    - **GitHub**
    - **GitLab**
    - **Bitbucket**
    - **Email and password**

    <Image
      src="https://docs.pipeops.io/img/docs/user-registration/sign-up-options.png"
      alt="Pipeops sign-up options showing GitHub, GitLab, Bitbucket, and Email buttons"
      width="1440"
      height="900"
    />

  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign up with email and password" icon="mail">

    If you choose **Email**, enter your basic details:

    - First name
    - Last name
    - Work email address
    - Password

    Then submit the form to create your account.

    <Image
      src="https://docs.pipeops.io/img/docs/user-registration/email-sign-up.png"
      alt="Email sign-up form with fields for first name, last name, email, and password"
      width="1440"
      height="900"
    />

  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify your email address" icon="check-circle">

    Check your inbox for a verification email from Pipeops. Enter the one-time passcode (OTP) from the email into the verification screen to confirm your address.

    <Image
      src="https://docs.pipeops.io/img/docs/user-registration/otp-verification.png"
      alt="OTP verification screen prompting for a one-time code sent by email"
      width="1440"
      height="900"
    />

    After successful verification, your account becomes active and you move on to workspace setup.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize your workspace" icon="settings">

    Personalize your workspace so Pipeops can tailor recommendations. You are asked a few short questions:

    - **Workspace name** (for example, your team or company name)
    - **Role or job title** (how you work with deployments)
    - **Primary goal** (what you want to achieve with Pipeops)
    - **How you heard about Pipeops** or similar discovery question

    <Image
      src="https://docs.pipeops.io/img/docs/user-registration/workspace-customization.png"
      alt="Workspace customization screen asking for workspace name, role, goals, and discovery source"
      width="1440"
      height="900"
    />

    Submit the form to save your workspace preferences.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete registration" icon="check">

    Confirm the details and finish the registration flow. You are redirected into the console, ready to configure how you want to deploy your applications.

    <Image
      src="https://docs.pipeops.io/img/docs/user-registration/registration-complete.png"
      alt="Confirmation screen showing successful account creation and a button to continue to the console"
      width="1440"
      height="900"
    />

    When you see the console dashboard, your registration is complete and your workspace is ready.

  </Step>
</Steps>
