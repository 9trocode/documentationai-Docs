---
title: Failed Deployment
description: Resolve deployment failures in Pipeops.
---

## Symptoms

- Deployments show "failed" status
- Error: "Build error" in logs

## Common Causes

- Invalid environment variables
- Missing Dockerfile or config

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check logs">
    ````bash
    # In Pipeops dashboard
    View build logs for error details.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Correct config">
    Ensure Dockerfile and config files exist and are valid.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Redeploy">
    Retry deployment after fixing issues.
  </Step>
</Steps>
