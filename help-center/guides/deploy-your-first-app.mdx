## Overview

Deploy any repo in minutes with Pipeops.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect repository">
    Authorize Pipeops to access your GitHub or GitLab account and select your repo.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure settings">
    Set required environment variables and choose a region.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start deployment">
    Click "Deploy" in the dashboard. Monitor logs for progress.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access your app">
    You'll receive a URL once deployment finishes successfully.
  </Step>
</Steps>
